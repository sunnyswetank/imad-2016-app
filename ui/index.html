About Me:

I am a Data Miner by profession. I work with raw datasets (e-commerce transactions, product reviews, travel bookings etc.) to find actionable insights. Most of these insights are predictions with an associated probability/confidence level. For example, in case of revenue management for a hotel, I may be asked to predict the demand for a given day or I may have to assess the likelihood that a customer will cancel his/her booking. If I am working with an e-commerce client, the client may be interesting in finding the best product recommendations for their customers. On the other hand, a bank may be interested in discovering a set of customers from its existing customer base who are most likely to adopt a recently launched premium travel credit card. I am sure you are familiar with some of these problem statements/ use cases. Very often, to present the results of my analysis, I have to do some kind of visualization as well to highlight the most important results. For analysis, I generally use a mix of R, SAS, Hive (on Hadoop) and for visualisation and presentation I may use PowerPoint or Tableau (usually when a live dashboard needs to be built).
