Who am I? Like really?

I am a Data Miner by profession. I work with raw datasets (e-commerce transactions, product reviews, travel bookings etc.) to find actionable insights. Most of these insights are predictions with an associated probability/confidence level. For example, in case of revenue management for a hotel, I may be asked to predict the demand for a given day or I may have to assess the likelihood that a customer will cancel his/her booking. If I am working with an e-commerce client, the client may be interesting in finding the best product recommendations for their customers. On the other hand, a bank may be interested in discovering a set of customers from its existing customer base who are most likely to adopt a recently launched premium travel credit card.

I am sure you are familiar with some of these problem statements/ use cases. Very often, to present the results of my analysis, I have to do some kind of visualization as well to highlight the most important results. For analysis, I generally use a mix of R, SAS, Hive (on Hadoop) and for visualisation and presentation I may use PowerPoint or Tableau (usually when a live dashboard needs to be built).

What does this webapp do?

3.1. A menu bar on the top listing and linked to different sections (fixed in position) 3.2. Clicking any menu option should highlight the selection 3.3. The clicked section should scroll up/down to the top of the page just below the menu bar 3.4. The section should scroll up/down in the same amount of time each time a menu option is clicked 3.5. A comments section where any user can add a comment 3.6. This comment sections should be able to store all comments added by users in a database 3.7. A log in functionality for visitors to register themselves on the app 3.8. Show number of likes, dislikes for comments and provide option of sorting comments by date, likes etc.
